1XX – Informational

100 – Continue
101 – Switching protocol
102 – Processing
103 – Early hints

2XX – Success
200 – OK
201 – Created (For Post operation)
202 – Accepted
204 – No Content
206 – Partial Content

3XX – Redirection
301 – Moved Permanently
302 – Found
304 – Not modified
308 – Permanent Redirect

4XX – Client Error
400 – Bad request
401 – Unauthorized
403 – Forbidden
404 – Not Found
405 – Method not allowed
406 – Not Acceptable
429 – Too many requests (Used to indicate server throttling)

5XX – Server Error
500 – Server error
501 – Not implemented
502 – Bad gateway
503 – Service unavailable
